# KPI Framework — Amman Digital Market

Define 5 KPIs for the Amman Digital Market. At least 2 must be time-based and 1 must be cohort-based.

---

## KPI 1

- **Name:** Monthly Revenue Trend  
- **Definition:** Total revenue generated in each month to track business growth over time.  
- **Formula:** SUM(quantity × unit_price) grouped by month  
- **Data Source (tables/columns):** orders.order_date, order_items.quantity, products.unit_price  
- **Baseline Value:** Revenue fluctuates across months (see monthly_revenue.png)  
- **Interpretation:** Revenue is not consistent over time, which suggests that demand is unstable.  
  This could indicate seasonal behavior or inconsistent customer engagement.

---

## KPI 2


- **Name:** Monthly Order Volume  
- **Definition:** Number of unique orders placed each month.  
- **Formula:** COUNT(DISTINCT order_id) grouped by month  
- **Data Source (tables/columns):** orders.order_date, orders.order_id  
- **Baseline Value:** Order volume varies month-to-month (see monthly_orders.png)  
- **Interpretation:** Customer activity is not stable. Some months show higher engagement,  
  while others drop, which may indicate retention or demand issues.


---

## KPI 3

- **Name:** Average Order Value (AOV)  
- **Definition:** Average amount spent per order across all customers.  
- **Formula:** Total revenue ÷ total number of orders  
- **Data Source (tables/columns):** order_items.quantity, products.unit_price, orders.order_id  
- **Baseline Value:** 109.94 JOD  
- **Interpretation:** On average, customers spend around 110 JOD per order.  
  This is a moderate value, meaning increasing AOV could significantly improve revenue  
  without needing to acquire more customers.

---

## KPI 4

- **Name:** Revenue by City (Cohort KPI)  
- **Definition:** Total revenue generated from customers grouped by city.  
- **Formula:** SUM(line_total) grouped by city  
- **Data Source (tables/columns):**  customers.city, order_items.quantity, products.unit_price  
- **Baseline Value:**  
  Amman: 15,719 JOD  
  Irbid: 7,250 JOD  
  Zarqa: 4,842 JOD  
  Madaba: 4,691 JOD  
  Salt: 4,477 JOD  
  Aqaba: 4,167 JOD  
- **Interpretation:**  
  Amman clearly contributes the highest share of revenue.  
  However, this does not necessarily mean customers in Amman spend more —  
  it may simply reflect a larger customer base.

---

## KPI 5

- **Name:** Revenue by Product Category  
- **Definition:** Total revenue generated by each product category.  
- **Formula:** SUM(line_total) grouped by category  
- **Data Source (tables/columns):** products.category, order_items.quantity, products.unit_price  
- **Baseline Value:** Revenue distribution is uneven across categories (see charts)  
- **Interpretation:** Some product categories generate significantly more revenue than others.  
  This suggests that product mix plays a critical role in overall business performance.
