# Executive Summary — Amman Digital Market Analytics

## Top Findings

<!-- List 3-5 key findings from your analysis. Each finding should be one sentence
     that a non-technical stakeholder can understand. Support each with a specific
     number or statistic. -->


1. Amman generates the highest revenue at 15,719 JOD, more than double Irbid (7,250 JOD), making it the primary revenue driver.  

2. Product category has a major impact on revenue, with statistically significant differences between categories (p < 0.001).  

3. The average order value is approximately 110 JOD, indicating moderate customer spending per transaction.  

---

## Supporting Data

<!-- For each finding above, reference the specific KPI value, statistical test result,
     or visualization that supports it. Include chart filenames so the reader can
     locate the evidence. -->


- Finding 1 is supported by the "revenue_city.png" chart and KPI "Revenue by City".  
  The statistical test (t-test, p = 0.936) shows that the difference in spending between cities is not significant, meaning the higher revenue in Amman is likely due to more customers rather than higher spending per customer.

- Finding 2 is supported by "category_boxplot.png" and "heatmap.png".  
  The ANOVA test (p < 0.001) confirms that revenue differs significantly across product categories, indicating strong variation in performance.

- Finding 3 is supported by the KPI "Average Order Value (AOV)", calculated at 109.94 JOD.  
  This reflects consistent but moderate spending behavior across customers.

## Recommendations

<!-- Based on your findings, what actions should the business take?
     Each recommendation should be specific, actionable, and tied to a finding above. -->


1. Invest more in high-performing product categories  
   Since category differences are statistically significant, focusing on top-performing categories (e.g., promotions, inventory expansion) can directly increase revenue.

2. Expand customer acquisition outside Amman  
   Since spending behavior is similar across cities, growth should focus on increasing the number of customers in lower-performing regions like Aqaba and Salt.

3. Increase average order value through upselling strategies  
   Introduce bundles, discounts, or product recommendations to encourage customers to spend more per order.
